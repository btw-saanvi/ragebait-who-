# Level Devil

A brutally difficult platformer game where every jump matters and every mistake costs you. Navigate through treacherous levels filled with betraying platforms, invisible blocks, moving hazards, and deadly obstacles.

### Objective
Reach the glowing yellow exit door at the end of each level. Sounds simple? Think again.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Obstacles

### Betraying Platforms (Red)
- These platforms **break** shortly after you step on them
- They pulse red when activated
- Don't linger!

### Invisible Platforms (Gray, Semi-transparent)
- Hidden until you touch them
- Reveal themselves when stepped on
- Trust your memory

### Moving Platforms (Blue)
- Move horizontally back and forth
- Ride them to cross gaps
- Timing is crucial

### Rotating Saws (Red Circles)
- Spin continuously
- Instant death on contact
- Avoid at all costs

### Spikes (Red Triangles)
- Line the floor in dangerous areas
- One touch = death
- Watch your landing

### Ceilings (Dark Gray)
- Block vertical shortcuts
- Force you to use platforms
- No jumping over obstacles!

### Fake Doors (Green)
- Look like exits but move away when approached
- Don't be fooled

### Exit Door (Yellow, Pulsing)
- The real goal
- Glows to stand out
- Touch it to complete the level

## üíÄ Difficulty

This game is designed to be **extremely challenging**. Expect to die. A lot. The game is inspired by "Getting Over It" and other masocore platformers where precision and patience are key.

### Tips for Survival
1. **Study the level** - Look before you leap
2. **Time your jumps** - Moving platforms require precision
3. **Remember invisible platforms** - They stay visible once touched
4. **Watch for betraying platforms** - They break quickly after activation
5. **Don't rush** - Patience is your friend
6. **Learn from deaths** - Each death teaches you something
